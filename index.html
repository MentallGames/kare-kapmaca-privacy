Privacy Policy

Kare Kapmaca · MentallGames · Last updated: April 2026

Kare Kapmaca does not share your data with third parties and does not send any data to external servers over the internet.

1. Data We Collect

The app only stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Username (entered by you)
  • Game statistics (wins, losses, points)
  • App settings (language, theme, sound)

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

2. Advertising (AdMob)

The app displays ads via Google AdMob. Google AdMob may use device identifiers and advertising IDs for ad personalization. For users in the European Union, ad personalization is only performed with your explicit consent.

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

3. Third-Party Sharing

Your personal data is never shared, sold, or rented to any third party.

4. Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all age groups. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

5. Data Deletion

To delete your game data, simply uninstall the app from your device. All local data will be automatically deleted.
